Benefits

Being in nature can boost your mood and improve mental health. Spending quality
time in the great outdoors reduces stress, calms anxiety, and can lead to a lower risk of depression, according to a study done by researchers
at Stanford University. In addition to having
mental health benefits, being outdoors opens up your senses to your surroundings and improves your sensory perception. Taking in the sights, smells, and feelings of nature has so many health benefits it can even be prescribed by a doctor.
