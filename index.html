Benefits

Hiking offers numerous benefits, making it one of my favorite hobbies. It provides a full-body workout, strengthening muscles
and bones, improving balance, and boosting heart health. Mentally, it reduces stress, calms anxiety, and can lower depression risk.
Hiking also enhances sensory perception and builds stronger relationships when done with friends or family.
With over 400 national parks to explore, each hike presents unique opportunities to enjoy nature and improve overall well-being.
